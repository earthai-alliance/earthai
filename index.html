Earth Vision AI Alliance

Advancing AI and Computer Vision for Earth Observation

We focus on cutting-edge research in remote sensing, satellite imagery analysis, and AI-powered solutions for understanding our planet.

A joint research initiative between Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) and IBM Research, combining academic excellence with industry innovation to tackle the most challenging problems in geospatial AI and Earth observation.

üí° Projects

GeoVLMs
A comprehensive framework for geospatial vision-language models, including EarthDial for multi-sensory earth observations and GeoVLM-R1 for reinforcement learning-based reasoning in remote sensing tasks.
GEOBench-VLM
A comprehensive benchmarking framework for evaluating vision-language models on geospatial tasks, including temporal analysis, fine-grained object detection, damage assessment, and spatial reasoning capabilities.
EarthVision Agents
Agent frameworks for Earth AI that enable autonomous systems to perform complex Earth Observation tasks through structured reasoning, tool use, and multi-step problem solving in remote sensing applications.
